About

Minoru Kagenou, who strives to become strong secretly, pretends to be an average student by day but fights local biker gangs at night. After a near-fatal truck accident, he awakens in a magical world as Cid and forms "Shadow Garden" to combat the real Cult of Diablos, which he initially believed to be fictional.
